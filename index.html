Shan Ji

I am an Undergraduate Researcher focused on studying tumor adaptation and metastasis, particularly translational heterogeneity during lung and bone colonization. I analyze spatial transcriptomics data to explore gene-cell interactions and the genomic changes that drive tumor progression in new environments. Using dimensional reduction and deconvolution methods, I investigate the biological pathways involved in metastasis.

Additionally, I am a Machine Learning Fellow at MIT's Break Through Tech AI program, where I build models to address business challenges. I am also working with the Center for Nonprofit Management to develop an NLP-powered chatbot that streamlines user inquiries based on organizational data.

My expertise spans spatial transcriptomics, machine learning, data analysis, software development, feature engineering, and instructional design.
